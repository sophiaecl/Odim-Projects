Breakup Salad Recipe

Madeup Recipe


A few months ago I was on a big salad kick. The weather was beautiful in Madrid, Spring started really early this year. Spring time is salad time. I ended up making this salad and became obsessed. When the guy I was in love with came to visit me in Madrid a few weeks later and I made it for him. He loved the salad so much that he went home and immediately made it. He made it for his friends and his family he thought it was that good. That's when I thought I had made my own version of the "Marry Me Chicken" (I don't eat meat). Boy was I wrong. After that trip I was head over heels in love, thinking I was going to marry this boy. Well a week after coming back from Madrid things ended. So now it is the complete opposite of my own "Marry Me Chicken". But it's still really delicious, maybe just bad luck.


Ingredients

  • One medium sized tomato, diced
  • One small purple onion, diced
  • Half a large cucumber, diced
  • One red pepper, diced
  • 1/4 of a medium cabbage, diced
  • One can of black beans, rinsed
  • Half a can of sweet corn
  • 1 cup of cooked and cooled quinoa
  • 1/3 cup of cilantro
  • 1/3 cup of crumbled feta cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • 2 teaspoons of peppeer
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of powdered cayenne pepper
  • 1 and 1/2 tablespoons of nutitional yeast
  • 1 tablspoon of chia seeds
  • 1 tablespoon of hemp seeds

Instructions

  1. Mix all the chopped veggies with the beans, corn, cilantro, and quinoa.
  2. Pour the apple cider vinegar and olive oil on top.
  3. Season.
  4. Add seeds.
  5. Add cheese.
  6. Mix well and serve or chill in the fridge.
